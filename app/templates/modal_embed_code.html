What is a GMC Appraisal Cycle and Revalidation?

Revalidation is the process by which the GMC ensures doctors remain fit to practise. Every licensed doctor must undergo this process every five years.

Your revalidation is based on a series of annual appraisals that form a core part of demonstrating that you meet the standards in the GMCâ€™s Good Medical Practice.

The period of 5 years is known as an appraisal cycle. At the end of this cycle, your Responsible Officer will submit a recommendation to the GMC for your revalidation.

Once revalidated, a new five-year appraisal cycle begins.
